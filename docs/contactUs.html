Reporting tools

Bugs should be reported by using the Apache Bug Database. Before reporting a problem, please check the project history file to see if the problem is already known.

Support requests should be sent to the log4j-user list (see below).

Mailing lists

New releases and important bug-fixes are anounced at the log4j-anouncement list. You can subscribe to the log4j-announce list, or change your existing subscription online. The traffic on this list is extremely low and you are encouraged to subscribe to this list if you are using log4j.

Discussion on log4j and logging in general are held on the log4j-user mailing list. To post a message to all its members, send email to log4j-user@jakarta.apache.org.

To subscribe to the log4j-users list send an email to log4j-user-subscribe@jakarta.apache.org.

Development related discussions are held on the log4j-dev mailing list. To post a message to all its members, send email to log4j-dev@jakarta.apache.org.

To subscribe to the log4j-users list send an email to log4j-dev-subscribe@jakarta.apache.org.
